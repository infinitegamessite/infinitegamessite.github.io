Open House Contemporary offers a unique vision on the role of art in the life of the viewer, home, city and the community. By bringing together a diverse collection of contemporary artworks in residential settings, OHC places art front and center in our lives, while at the same time recognizing that art can be just as compelling in a living room as in a gallery.
