MARVIN TATE

Marvin Tate is a noted Chicago artist, whose assemblage work is charged with memories and unspoken truths about life, hope and the passage of time. By juxtaposing found objects in boxes, crates and wooden drawers, Tate creates a poetic realm where desolate landscapes, abandoned buildings, empty rooms and peculiar figures personify the beauty in obscure settings.
