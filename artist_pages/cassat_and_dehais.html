CASSAT AND DEHAIS

Hugo, a former mathematician, his cousin Vincent and Matthias, a cabinet-maker, joined forces with common passions: contemporary furniture, ornamental and cabinetmaking. They set up their studio in Avezan, a village of about fifty inhabitants, in the Gers, France. For them, nothing is more precious than the hands of the two cabinetmakers. No machine can match the quasi-surgical work and precision of their hands.
