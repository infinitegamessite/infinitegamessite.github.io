PATRICIA EVANS

Patricia Evans is a photographer based in Chicago.

She has exhibited and published widely. Her past work includes a photo essay on Gypsy families in France, and a study of storefront churches on the South Side of Chicago. She documented the construction of Millennium Park and life in high-rise public housing. For 10 years she has photographed survivors of rape for The Voices and Faces Project. Her series on sexual assault, an installation of twenty-five photographs, is in the collection of the Museum of Contemporary Photography.
