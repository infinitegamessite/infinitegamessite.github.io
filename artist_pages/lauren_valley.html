LAUREN VALLEY

Lauren Valley is a sculptor and new media artist based in Pittsburgh, PA. Currently, she is pursuing her BFA in Fine Arts with a minor in Physical Computing from Carnegie Mellon University. Lauren's work combines sculpture and robotics to create reactive systems that force the user to create symbiotic relationships with technology. Her work has been featured in the United States, the United Kingdom, and Spain, including Tate Modern’s “Future Late,” The Carnegie Museum of Art’s “2 Minute Film Festival,” and the Sun Pier House. Lauren completed a residency at Massachusetts Museum of Contemporary Art (MASS MoCA) in 2016, and has recently been granted an artist residency at ACRE, July 2017.
