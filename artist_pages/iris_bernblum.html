IRIS BERNBLUM

Iris Bernblum (b. 1975) is a cross-disciplinary artist. Using a range of media, including video, photography, drawing and sculpture, Bernblum explores ideas around tension and release, power and play, fantasy and escape. Her work plays with the boundaries between what is considered normal and abnormal, as well as carefully and quietly subverting the viewer. Bernblum has shown at Aspect/Ratio Gallery, Terrain Exhibitions, and Weinberg/Newton Gallery in Chicago.
